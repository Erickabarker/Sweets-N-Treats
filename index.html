About Us

We are a family(pets too) friendly shop with sit in and take-away. We specialize in cupcakes, milkshakes and traybakes.

Our aim is to provide you with yummy indulgent treats in a comfortable environment. Any spend over £10 you get a free treat for your furry friends.
